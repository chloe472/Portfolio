Design Process & Inspiration

1

Research & Inspiration

My design process for this migrant worker advocacy project focused on creating accessible, empathetic visual communications that could effectively raise awareness about working conditions while maintaining dignity and respect for the subject matter.


Key Inspirations:

  • Modern Advocacy Campaigns: Bold, approachable layouts with oversized sans-serif text optimized for social media engagement
  • Empathy-Driven Imagery: Heart motifs, hand shapes, and thoughtful character illustrations to highlight care and human connection
  • Minimalist Communication: Clean layouts with plenty of whitespace ensuring key messaging is easily absorbed
  • Abstract Organic Shapes: Wavy forms and gentle curves to suggest warmth, inclusivity, and approachability
2

Color System Development

I developed a warm, accessible color palette that prioritizes readability while conveying empathy and hope. The colors were chosen to be uplifting yet respectful of the serious advocacy theme.

Color Strategy:

  • Warm Cream (#FFF9EC): Soft, neutral backgrounds providing warmth and excellent readability
  • Coral Red (#FF6F61): Energetic accent for hand motifs, hearts, and key visual elements
  • Soft Yellow (#FFF7B2): Background shapes that add positivity and light to the compositions
  • Teal Green (#2CB57A): Abstract leaves and calming elements introducing balance
  • Blush Pink (#F8BBD0): Gentle contrast shapes for visual interest without overwhelming
  • Muted Blue (#8AD3E6): Accent color for figure clothing and text highlights
  • Black (#000): Primary text color ensuring maximum contrast and accessibility
3

Typography & Visual Consistency

Typography was structured to guide readers through advocacy messaging while maintaining a friendly, accessible tone that respects both the audience and the subject matter.

Montserrat Bold - Headlines & Key Questions

Poppins Regular - Body Text & Explanations

  • Montserrat Bold: Large, impactful headers for key questions and advocacy statements
  • Poppins Regular: Clean, geometric sans-serif for body copy ensuring high legibility
  • Emphasis Strategy: Key words like "help," "know," and "transfer" weighted extra-bold for focus
  • Hierarchy System: Big headline questions, medium-weight explanations, and color-coded accent words
  • Accessibility Focus: High contrast between text and backgrounds for maximum readability
