The Challenge

It's Raining Raincoats (IRR) is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting migrant workers in Singapore. Their existing booking system faced critical operational challenges that hindered their mission.

My Contribution

As UI/UX Designer, I designed iterative low-fi and high-fi prototypes with Figma, integrating user and stakeholder feedback to enhance usability. I defined the product vision, utilising tools like FigmaMake for rapid prototyping and requirements analysis.
