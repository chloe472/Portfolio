The Challenge

ItsRainingRaincoats (IRR) current booking system cannot block repeat bookings or track eligibility, and lacks a central dashboard for staff. Communication with workers is manual and no integrated worker’s history, making it difficult to manage attendance, capacity and follow-ups.

Objectives

Design a book and management platform that prevents repeat bookings, automates confirmations and reminders, and integrates worker history for better tracking and service delivery.

Users

Types of Users Description Implication
1. IRR Administrative Staff
(Ashwini)
The administrative person who is in charge of handling all CRUD operations regarding timeslots of the weekend shop and handling responses posed by migrant workers.
  • Eliminate the need for manual checking of migrant workers to see if they meet the requirements for visiting the weekend shop (6-month rule, work permit check, gender).
  • Increase the ease of viewing, editing, creating, and cancelling timeslot bookings.
  • Eliminate the need of manually sending confirmation messages.
  • Easily view attendance records of migrant workers for each slot.
  • Ease of creating and publishing timeslots and reminder message.
  • Easily view the details and records of migrant workers.
2. IRR Weekend Shop Helper
The individual(s) tasked with managing the weekend shop where the migrant workers patronise.
  • Easily view attendance records of migrant workers for each slot.
  • Manually input worker's attendance or scan worker's QR code to indicate their attendance in the system.
