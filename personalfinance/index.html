Focus

PocketPal Expense Capture Flow

The Challenge

PocketPal's expense capture flow needed to make a repetitive financial task feel simple and reliable: users should be able to scan a receipt, review extracted details, and confirm an expense without losing confidence in the data.

UX Priorities

Low-friction capture: Reduce steps between receipt scanning and expense review.

Clear confirmation: Let users verify details before saving transactions.

Error prevention: Apply heuristic thinking to reduce ambiguity and mistakes.

Complete flow: Show the end-to-end journey through structured wireframes.

My Solution

I designed mid-fidelity wireframe flows for PocketPal's expense capture feature, mapping the journey from receipt scanning to expense confirmation with structured layouts and functional UI elements.

The flow emphasized clarity, reviewability, and user control so the financial-recording interaction could feel trustworthy rather than tedious.
