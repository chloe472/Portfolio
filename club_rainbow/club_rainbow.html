Design Process & Inspiration

1

Research & Inspiration

My design process for this project was rooted in creating a friendly and engaging visual storytelling experience, combining playful illustrations with local cultural references to make learning about Singapore accessible for children with chronic illnesses.


Key Inspirations:

  • Character-Driven Illustration: Inspired by approachable and playful cartoon styles to create warmth and accessibility
  • Singaporean Cultural Elements: Local icons and landmarks that ground the experience in familiar Singaporean context
  • Narrative Flow Design: Comic-inspired slide layouts to keep engagement through visual storytelling
2

Color System Development

I developed a vibrant and diverse color palette reflecting the dynamic and multicultural spirit of Singapore, balancing accessibility and emotional connection across all materials.

Color Strategy:

  • Sky Blue (#71C7F5): Used for backgrounds and highlights, evoking openness and optimism in the storytelling
  • Sunshine Yellow (#FFE03D): Applied in speech bubbles and callouts to bring warmth and attention to key messages
  • Royal Purple (#7E55C7): Accent color representing creativity and cultural richness
  • Coral Pink (#F55072): Used for interactive elements and emotional highlights to create energy and engagement
  • Leaf Green (#44BF6D): Symbolizes growth, health, and community support
  • Vibrant Orange (#FF8C42): Highlights action buttons and important notifications to inspire activity
  • Bright Red-Orange (#FF5A36): Used sparingly for warnings and important alerts without overwhelming users
  • Deep Navy (#284C7E): Primary text and header color, ensuring clarity and professionalism
3

Typography & Consistency

Typography is structured to guide the reader clearly through the content while reflecting the friendly and inclusive tone of the project.

Montserrat Bold - Headers & Main Titles

Nunito SemiBold - Body Text & Content

  • Montserrat: Bold and modern for strong, clear headings that anchor each section
  • Nunito: Warm, semi-bold body font lending readability and approachability for content
  • Comic Neue: Used exclusively in speech bubbles to convey playfulness and dialogue emphasis in a conversational tone
  • Consistent Hierarchy: Clear typographic structure ensures users follow the narrative smoothly across media
