The Challenge

It's Raining Raincoats (IRR) is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting migrant workers in Singapore. Their existing booking system faced critical operational challenges that hindered their mission.

Key Problems Identified:

❌ No Repeat Booking Prevention: System couldn't block duplicate bookings, leading to capacity issues and unfair access

❌ Manual Communication: Staff had to manually send confirmations and reminders, consuming valuable time

❌ Lack of Worker History: No integrated tracking of attendance or eligibility, making follow-ups difficult

❌ No Central Dashboard: Staff lacked visibility into bookings, attendance, and capacity management
