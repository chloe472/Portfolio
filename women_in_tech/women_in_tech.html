Design Process & Inspiration

1

Research & Inspiration

I drew inspiration from pixel art aesthetics and tech-themed websites to create a cohesive visual identity that resonates with our computing community while maintaining a playful, approachable feel.

Key Inspirations:

  • Pixel Art: Clean geometric shapes and vibrant color blocks that create a tech-nostalgic atmosphere
  • Tech Websites: Modern layouts with clear information hierarchy and engaging visual elements
  • Gaming Aesthetics: Playful UI elements that make technology feel accessible and fun
  • Minimalist Design: Clean compositions that let content shine while maintaining visual interest
2

Color System Development

I developed a cohesive color palette that represents our three core pillars while maintaining accessibility and visual harmony across all materials.

Color Strategy:

  • Purple (#9796cf): Primary brand color representing innovation and creativity in tech
  • Cream (#fef9ef): Warm background color - welcoming, inclusive vibe
  • Charcoal (#393e41): Professional text color ensuring readability
  • Light Blue (#b3e1e6): Advocacy pillar - representing clarity and transparency
  • Coral (#ff9999): Career pillar - symbolizing growth and opportunity
  • Soft Yellow (#ffd997): Community pillar - conveying warmth and connection
3

Typography & Consistency

Establishing a clear typographic hierarchy with complementary fonts ensures readability while adding personality to our brand identity.

Poppins - Primary Content & Body Text

Nineby Five - Headers & Accent Elements

Typography Strategy:

  • Poppins: Modern, friendly sans-serif used for all body text, captions, and general content for optimal readability
  • Nineby Five: Pixel-style accent font for headers, titles, and special elements that reinforces our tech/gaming aesthetic
  • Consistent Hierarchy: Modular typography scale creates visual harmony across Instagram posts, presentations, and digital materials
