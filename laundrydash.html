Problem Statement

Busy urban professionals and families in Singapore often lack the time for household chores, especially laundry. While traditional laundromats exist, they require customers to drop off and pick up their clothes, which is inconvenient. There is a clear market opportunity for a technology-driven, on-demand laundry and dry-cleaning service that offers the convenience of modern delivery apps.

Our Solution

We designed a three-sided marketplace platform with AI-powered garment scanning for customers, optimized routing and tiered incentives for drivers, and automated order management for laundry partners, all coordinated through a comprehensive admin portal.

LaundryDash App

A mobile app that lets customers quickly order laundry services through AI-powered garment scanning, manage flexible subscription plans, and track orders in real-time from pickup through delivery.

LaundryDash Driver App

A mobile app that provides drivers with optimized multi-stop routes, shift booking up to 11 days in advance, daily automatic payouts, and a tiered incentive system that rewards high performers with better pay and exclusive benefits.
