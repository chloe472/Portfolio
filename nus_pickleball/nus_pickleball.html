Design Process & Inspiration

1

Research & Inspiration

I drew inspiration from dynamic sports branding, playful scrapbook aesthetics, and approachable social media layouts to create a cohesive visual identity for NUS Pickleball. The goal was to combine energetic athletic themes with engaging, student-friendly presentation.

Key Inspirations:

  • Sport Branding: Bold typography, high-contrast layouts, and icons (e.g., paddle, ball, silhouette players) to communicate energy and school spirit
  • Scrapbook & Collage Style: Torn-paper overlays, hand-drawn doodles, and sticker-like elements create a personal and approachable vibe, making information feel inviting and accessible
2

Color System Development

I developed a color palette rooted in our club's key references: the courts we play on, the sport itself, and NUS's iconic identity. These colors ensure the materials are eye-catching, relatable, and recognisably "NUS Pickleball."

Color Strategy:

  • Green (#4f6f52): Main background shade evoking the physical pickleball courts on campus
  • NUS Blue (#305a81): Used for headings, accents, and logo highlights reflecting NUS colours
  • NUS Orange (#ff914d): Draws attention to calls-to-action, program dates/titles
  • Pickleball Yellow (#cfd01a): Signature hue for pickleball balls, used as accents and icons
  • Line/Scrapbook Grey (#e4e5e6): Court line detail and scrapbook/collage elements for balance
  • Black (#000000): Key text, silhouettes, and sport icons for clarity and visual punch

These shades are consistently used across all posters, tying together the merch promotions, tournament events, and session advertisements.

3

Typography & Consistency

Typography was chosen to maximise both personality (for headlines/collages) and clarity (for key event details), ensuring harmony between playful and functional information.

boyrun - Headlines & Collage Text

Open Sans - NUS Pickleball Logo

Glacial Indifference - Informational Text

Typography Strategy:

  • boyrun: Used for collage-style text, headlines, and handwritten accent elements on scrapbook scraps for a carefree, energetic student vibe (seen in titles like "RACKETHLON 2025")
  • Open Sans: Chosen for the NUS Pickleball logo, providing a modern, friendly, and professional feel that compliments institutional branding
  • Glacial Indifference: Used for all informational text—dates, session times, and locations— clean, & keeps key details distinct

Design Approach:

  • Header and accent typography (boyrun) injects personality into each design, convey personal touch
  • The Open Sans logo ensures the club's brand remains approachable and easily recognisable
  • Glacial Indifference creates a professional, accessible impression for important details such as registration deadlines, costs, and venues
